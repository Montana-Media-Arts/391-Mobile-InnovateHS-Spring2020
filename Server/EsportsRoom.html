The Griz Esports Program in an incredible opportunity for students at the University of Montana that came to the school Fall 2019.
It grants students the oppurtinity to play video games competitively or for fun, and it features a no-cut program.
All of the people in this program are incredibley friendly and come from majors all over campus.
If you've ever thought of gaming on a real team that's like family, definitley check this place out!
